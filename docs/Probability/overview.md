# Probability

Sample spaces, distributions, conditional probability, and the stochastic processes that model price movement.

## What probability means for a quant

Probability is the native language of quantitative finance. A price isn't a number you predict — it's a random variable you characterize. Option pricing is an expectation under a carefully chosen measure; risk is a tail probability; a trading edge is a distribution shifted slightly in your favor and repeated thousands of times. Get comfortable thinking in distributions rather than point forecasts and most of the field stops looking like magic.
